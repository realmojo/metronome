## 출시 노트

### <en-US>

**Metronome & Chords - Initial Release**

Welcome to Metronome & Chords! This is the first release of our professional music practice app.

**Features:**

- Precision metronome with BPM range from 20 to 300
- Intuitive tempo control with slider and buttons
- Time signature settings (Beat/bar and Click/beat)
- 15 classic tempo markings (Larghissimo to Prestissimo)
- Visual beat indicator
- Chord practice tool with 8 chord types
- Support for all 12 chromatic notes
- Tension additions (9, 11, 13)
- Preset saving for both metronome and chord practice
- Dark mode interface
- Haptic feedback on iOS

Start your music practice journey with accurate tempo and systematic chord learning!

</en-US>
